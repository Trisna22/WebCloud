Your data is our priority

To protect your personal files we will encrypt them with a encryption algorithm to your choise. To view your files, your files will be decrypted with a key you can create in the beginning. This key can be a file or a pass-phrase. Note that, if you lose your key, you lose your data.

The choise of encryption algorithms are:

  • AES 128
  • AES 192
  • AES 256
  • More will be implemented

Our purpose

We do all this to protect our rights of privacy and freedom. We are not responsible for your actions and what data you are storing. We also don't want to know what the content of your data is, so that we won't get accused of something illegal. The only person who can see your files are the keepers of the key to the vault. No government, hackers or other companies are able to view your files. You have the right to delete your account and data, when you want to, all the data will be erased from our servers and systems. There is no turning back from then.

We are simply the vaultkeepers.
